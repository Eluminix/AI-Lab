Bollywood is all set to entertain all year round with a promising slate of films. While 2019 saw several female-oriented dramas and unexpected narratives win hearts, 2020 will be no different. Bollywood has packed a lot of real life stories, light-hearted romantic films as well as engaging horror dramas for fans this year. From Deepika Padukone’s acid attack survivor narrative to Ayushmann Khurrana’s homesexual love story – 2020 has several out-of-the-box ideas and here’s a list of 20 films you must watch this year!
Chhapaak

73045791

Deepika Padukone plays an acid attack survivor in ‘Chhapaak’, which is directed by Meghna Gulzar.
The film will hit the screens on January 10. Deepika has also turned producer with this venture, which also features Vikrant Massey in a pivotal role.
Tanhaji – The Unsung Warrior

73045800

Ajay Devgn plays the lead in this war drama which is based on an iconic Maratha battle. Ajay will fight Saif Ali Khan and play out the historical Battle of Sinhagad on the silver screen. ‘Tanhaji – The Unsung Warrior’ will hit the screens on January 10 and will clash with ‘Chhapaak’ at the box office.
Love Aaj Kal 2

73045832

Millennial lovers Sara Ali Khan and Kartik Aaryan will paint the silver screen with their romance in this film. Tentatively titled ‘Love Aaj Kal 2’, the film is directed by Imtiaz Ali. Sara and Kartik began dating while working on this film which is slated for release on Valentine’s Day, February 14.
Shubh Mangal Zyada Saavdhan

73045846

Trust Ayushmann Khurrana to surprise every time he graces the silver screen. 2020 will see the actor romance another male actor in ‘Shubh Mangal Zyada Saavdhan’ which is touted to be a light-hearted gay love story. Jitendra Kumar, who has featured in several YouTube sketches, has been cast opposite Ayushmann in the film. ‘Shubh Mangal Zyada Saavdhan’ is slated for release on February 21.
Gunjan Saxena – The Kargil Girl

73045853

Janhvi Kapoor has taken up the responsibility to narrate the story of India’s first female Air Force Pilot’s tale and how she flew in the combat area to save Indian soldiers. Backed by Karan Johar, ‘Gunjan Saxena – The Kargil Girl’ promises to paint a real life story and will hit the screens on March 13.

Angrezi Medium

73045937

Irrfan Khan returns to entertain making ‘Angrezi Medium’ a must watch on your list. The film will feature a came by Kareena Kapoor Khan and we can’t wait to watch what the makers have in store. ‘Angrezi Medium’ will release on March 20.
Sooryavanshi

73045984

Akshay Kumar has joined Rohit Shetty’s cop universe and the results will be out for everyone to watch on March 27. ‘Sooryavanshi’ will feature a special sequence for fans as Sigham aka Ajay Devgn and Simmba aka Ranveer Singh have also shot for a cameo in the film.
‘83’

73046007

Ranveer Singh and Deepika Padukone will return to entertain together after marriage with ‘83’. The film will capture the Indian cricket team’s iconic win at the 1983 world cup. Directed by Kabir Khan, ‘83’ is slated for release on April 10.
Gulabo Sitabo

73046043

The makers of ‘Gulabo Sitabo’ managed to pull a casting coup with powerhouse performers Amitabh Bachchan and Ayushmann Khurrana sharing the frame. The film promises a quirky narrative, which will hit the screens on April 17.
Coolie No 1

73046050

Varun Dhawan will try to recreate the charm of Govinda in the present day adaption of ‘Coolie No 1’. This colourful drama features Sara Ali Khan opposite Varun and is scheduled to release on May 1.
Brahmastra

73046064

Bollywood’s hottest couple, Ranbir Kapoor and Alia Bhatt will romance on the silver screen in this super hero drama, directed by Ayan Mukerji. ‘Brahmastra’ also features Amitabh Bachchan, Mouni Roy and Nagarjuna in pivotal roles. The film doesn’t have a final release date yet but the makers plan to launch the movie in Summer 2020.
Shakuntala Devi – Human Computer

73046069

Vidya Balan is known for expertly portraying real life characters and in 2020 the actress will step into the garbs of celebrated mathematician Shakuntala Devi. Directed by Anu Memon the film will feature Sanya Malhotra as Vidya’s daughter. ‘Shakuntala Devi – Human Computer’ will hit the screens on May 8.
Laxmmi Bomb

73046078

After ‘Sooryavanshi’, Akshay Kumar will be seen playing a transgender character and his saree-clad first look poster of ‘Laxmmi Bomb’ was met with a huge applause. The film is a remake of a South hit and will release on May 22, banking on Eid 2020.
Radhe: Your Most Wanted Bhai

73046091

Salman Khan will return with his action packed avatar in ‘Radhe: Your Most Wanted Bhai’. The film is set to lock horns at the box office with Akshay Kumar starrer ‘Laxmmi Bomb’ on Eid 2020.
Thalaivi

73046116

Kangana Ranaut will portray powerful politician, late Jayalalitha, on the silver screen this year in her biopic titled ‘Thalaivi’. The film promises an insight into the life of the actress turned Chief Minister of Tamil Nadu. ‘Thalaivi’ will release on June 26.
Bhool Bhulaiyaa 2

73046136

A sequel to the much entertaining horror comedy, ‘Bhool Bhulaiyaa 2’ features millennial actors – Kartik Aaryan and Kiara Advani in the lead. The film sees Kartik essay the role of a ghost buster. ‘Bhool Bhulaiyaa 2’ is scheduled to hit the screens on July 31.
Jersey

73046156

After ‘Kabir Singh’, Shahid Kapoor signed up for yet another South remake. The actor will be seen playing a retired cricketer in ‘Jersey’, which is a remake of Telugu sports drama. ‘Jersey’ director Gowtham Tinnanuri is calling the shots for the Bollywood remake, which will release on August 28.
Gangubai Kathiawadi

73046175

Alia Bhatt’s most anticipated film of 2019 is ‘Gangubai Kathiawadi’ wherein she will be directed by ace filmmaker Sanjay Leela Bhansali. The actress has already started with diction lessons for her role, as she plays a brothel madam. SLB has announced to release ‘Gangubai Kathiawadi’ on September 11.
Dhaakad

73046187

Kangana Ranaut left everyone speechless with her action packed avatar in ‘Dhaakad’. The actress stunned all with her gun wielding teaser. The action entertainer will feature Kangana a fierce avatar, ‘Dhaakad’ is slated for release in October.
Sardar Udham Singh


After his impactful performance in ‘Uri: The Surgical Strike’, Vicky Kaushal will return with yet another real life story in 2020. The actor will play the lead role in Sardar Udham Singh biopic, narrating the journey of a fierce freedom fighter. Directed by Shoojit Sicar, the film is scheduled to hit the screens on October 2.
