Serie C player has Coronavirus

BySusy Campanale

Mar 10, 2020 21:24 coronavirus, Serie C, Vis Pesaro

Serie C side Vis Pesaro have announced one of their players has tested positive for the Coronavirus.

A statement from the club confirmed a member of the squad had been diagnosed, even though he currently had no symptoms.

It’s not yet clear if they will isolate the whole team or just this one player.

The Serie C tournament has been suspended anyway until April 3 due to the Coronavirus outbreak that has hit over 10,000 people in Italy.
