Venezuelan president congratulates 41st anniversary of Islamic revolution

Feb 12, 2020, 2:56 PM
News ID: 83672284

Tehran, Feb 12, IRNA – Venezuelan President Nicolás Maduro congratulated his Iranian counterpart Hassan Rouhani on the occasion of the 41st anniversary of the victory of the 1979 Islamic Revolution.

Stressing the fraternal relations between Tehran and Caracas, President Maduro tweeted that the path of strengthening ties and mutual respect is to be continued; the path will help the two countries in attaining unified peace.  

He said that Iran and Venezuela have long had good relations in different fields and been under oppressive pressures of the US.
