What is the house edge?

The house edge is the advantage the casino has over the players. This advantage is different for every game, but the casino always has this advantage resulting in getting more money from the players than it pays out as winnings. For example, in blackjack the casino has an edge because the dealer always wins when the player busts, even if he would also have bust. House edge is the advantage expressed in percentage. When it comes to gambling machines, the term RTP is used. This is a very important characteristic as it shows how much money the machine pays out in terms of the summary of all bets.
